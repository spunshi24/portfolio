Trading in Markets Under Simulation

TiMUS — Your Financial Hub

TiMUS is a zero-risk simulator across stocks, crypto, forex, commodities, and ETFs. Built as my project, it mirrors real market conditions so people can practice strategy, discipline, and risk management without financial exposure.

Why TiMUS

During COVID, I learned markets alongside my dad—we even paper-traded with accounting-style ledgers to balance P&L by hand. That experience sparked TiMUS: a structured, educational simulator that keeps the realism of market mechanics while removing financial risk.

Features include deliberate (Classic) and fast-paced (Turbo) modes, simulated multi-asset markets, real-time charts, and education modules on strategies, chart types, risk management, and banking basics. The goal is to learn by doing—not by losing.
